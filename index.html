STORY OF PLANPIX

We're passionate about bringing spaces to life.

Together with you!

Planpix have been helping people market their properties for over 10 years now, and we are very proud of the excellent reputation we have developed as a professional and friendly business.

We started out offering EPCs to a small number of clients in and around Bexley. Word soon spread and as our reputation grew we began to add floor plans and high end photography.

We now offer a one-stop-shop to estate agents and property owners alike by producing stunning photographs, a floor plan, an Energy Performance and a virtual tour in a single visit.
